COMBAT

Combat encounters should feel tense, tactical, and grounded.

Enemies behave intelligently.
They may:
- use cover
- reposition
- retreat if wounded
- coordinate with allies
- target perceived weaknesses

Combat is not static.

Environment matters:
- terrain
- obstacles
- visibility
- weather
- elevation

Avoid repetitive attack descriptions.
Use dynamic motion and consequence.

Not every conflict must become combat.
NPCs may threaten, negotiate, intimidate, or attempt escape.
